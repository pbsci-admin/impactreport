DECEMBER 2021

Top stories of the year: In this month's issue of the Impact Report, we are celebrating our top stories (the ones that got the most attention) and major honors from 2021. Thank you to everyone in the science community for continuing to push toward excellence even in the midst of a pandemic and providing us a foundation to start out strong in 2022.

Our Top Stories of the Year

OCEAN SCIENCES, UCSC NEWSCENTER

The collapse of Northern California kelp forests will be hard to reverse

Our research on the collapse of Northern California kelp forests was the most viewed news article for campus this year. The story was covered by multiple news outlets including the San Francisco Chronicle, CBS Sacramento, the Associated Press, NPR's Morning Edition, Smithsonian Magazine, and the New York Times.
