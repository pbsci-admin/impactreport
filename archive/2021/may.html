ECOLOGY & EVOLUTIONARY BIOLOGY, UCSC NEWSCENTER

Demonstrating the importance of regional-scale field research

In 2015, the late Barry Sinervo and his colleague Laurel Fox established the Institute for the Study of Ecological and Evolutionary Climate Impacts (ISEECI) to leverage UC Natural Reserve System to study. A new study demonstrates the importance of these types of research projects for tracking the effects of climate change.
