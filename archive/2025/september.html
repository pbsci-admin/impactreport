UC Santa Cruz ranks among top 25 global public universities in new rankings

Coming in at No. 24 among U.S. public universities and No. 47 among all U.S. universities, UC Santa Cruz also earned exceptional subject-specific rankings in several research areas: The campus ranked No. 12 in the world for space science and No. 7 among U.S. universities, and ranked No. 13 globally in marine and freshwater biology and second among U.S. universities.

OCTOBER 3  | 12:30–7:30 p.m. | STEVENSON EVENT CENTER
Environmental Health and Justice: Successes and Future Challenges

Please join the UC Santa Cruz Microbiology & Environmental Toxicology Department and Global and Community Health Program for a collaborative, interdisciplinary symposium to highlight and celebrate the important work being done in Global and Community Health at UCSC. The symposium will feature talks on environmental determinants of health by faculty, graduate students, and undergraduates. The keynote will be given by environmental health luminary Brenda Eskenazi from UC Berkeley.
