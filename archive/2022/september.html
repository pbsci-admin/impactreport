Webb Telescope sees a carbon dioxide atmosphere way out there

On August 25 a team of astrophysicists using the new telescope to probe exoplanets added to what has been a cavalcade of discoveries when they announced that they had detected carbon dioxide in the atmosphere of an exoplanet for the first time.
